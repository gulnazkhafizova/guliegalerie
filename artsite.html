Art Commissions

I'm delighted to offer custom art commissions! Whether you're looking for a personalized portrait, landscape, or abstract piece, I work closely with you to bring your vision to life.

What I Offer:

  • Custom portraits (people, pets, family)
  • Landscape and nature scenes
  • Abstract and contemporary art
  • Still life compositions
  • Various mediums: Acrylic, Watercolor, Oil

Process & Timeline:

The typical commission takes 2-4 weeks to complete, depending on size and complexity. I'll keep you updated throughout the creative process.

Payment:

I require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon completion before shipping.

Greetings and Welcome!

Hello! I'm Gulnaz Khafizova, a passionate artist based in Dubai. My journey with art began in childhood, and it has grown into a lifelong love affair with colors, textures, and creative expression.

I specialize in acrylic, watercolor, and oil paintings, drawing inspiration from the beauty of nature, human emotions, and the vibrant world around us. Each piece I create is a reflection of my inner world and an invitation for you to experience art in your own unique way.

Through my work, I aim to bring joy, beauty, and meaning into people's lives. Whether it's a peaceful landscape, a vibrant still life, or a custom commission, I pour my heart into every brushstroke.

Thank you for taking the time to visit my gallery. I hope my art resonates with you!
